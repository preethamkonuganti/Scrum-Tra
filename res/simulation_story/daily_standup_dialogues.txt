Scrum Ceremony - Daily Stand Up
Scenario: Usual conversation flow between a Scrum master and developers:

Scrum Master (SM): Good morning, team! How is everyone doing? Let's get started with our daily standup. Who wants to kick things off?

Developer 1 (D1): I'll go first. Yesterday, I completed ---task---

SM: Excellent, D1! What are you planning to work on today?

D1: Today, I'll be working on -- task --

SM: Sounds good. Any blockers or impediments?

D1: No blockers for me. Smooth sailing so far.
